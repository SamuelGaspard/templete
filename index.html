I am a freelance programmer, storyteller and outdoor lover from the Pacific Northwest. I consider myself lucky because I grew up surrounded by the mountains and ocean, with adventure on my doorstep. A love for the outdoors was instilled in me at a young age and only grew more once I started hiking, backpacking and traveling!

In 2024, I left my 9-to-5 job to put all my energy into exploring new countries, climbing mountains and capturing my experiences along the way. My sole mission is to inspire this community to get outdoors, explore and reap all the benefits nature offers.
